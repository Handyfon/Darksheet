Camp Flow (Overnight Rest)

1 Make Camp Everyone rolls Camping. No gear: disadvantage. At least one Survival check.
2 Lookout Secures camp. Failure gives disadvantage vs intruders.
3 Activities Non-lookouts perform one primary camp activity.
4 Sleep CON save vs activity DC. Bedroll/tent advantage, armor or lookout disadvantage.
5 Pack Up Put out the fire, strap on gear, and move out.

Rest Flow (Darker Dungeons)

  1. Outside camp: request camp roles, assign lookout, perform one camp activity, sleep (CON save), pack up.
  2. Long rest: at least 1 week in a sanctuary — town, village, or city.
  3. 1 Sell Loot · 2 Choose Lifestyle · 3 Primary Activity
  4. 4 Hear 3 Rumors · 5 Two Secondary Activities · 6 Settle Up
  5. On settle-up: apply lifestyle conditions (HP cap, HD %, hunger/thirst/fatigue), clear stress, roll Affliction Removal if relevant.

Gemcutting

Wisdom (jeweler's tools)

Roll Wisdom (jeweler's tools) vs the gem's quality DC. Success reveals the cut gem's full value and unlocks its magical use; failure shatters it into Arcane Dust worth half its uncut value.

Jewelcrafting

Arcana (jeweler's tools)

Roll Arcana (jeweler's tools) vs the tier DC (Royal 15 / Lucent 20 / Astral 25), using a cut Royal/Lucent/Astral gem of the recipe's type. Failure cracks the gem (it can no longer be crafted); a critical failure (−10) also drops its quality one step; a critical success (+10) yields spare Arcane Dust.

Alchemy - Oils

Arcana (alchemist's kit)

First Grind a cut Royal/Lucent/Astral elemental gem to dust (button in the Gemcutting panel), then select that dust here and roll Arcana (alchemist's kit) vs the tier DC (Royal 15 / Lucent 20 / Astral 25). Success yields 2 vials; a critical success (+10) yields 3; failure spoils the batch. The dust is consumed either way.

GM Tools — only you can see this

An item can have enchantments OR arcane sockets, not both. It holds up to its socket count in jewels (set per item; default 3, max 5), no two of the same type. Socketing takes 1 hour and proficiency with suitable tools — no roll; the item must be attuned for its jewels to work. Unsocketing (8 h) rolls Int (Arcana) vs the gem's DC (Royal 10 / Lucent 15 / Astral 20) — on a failure the jewel shatters; Shatter (1 h) always destroys it.
