Camp Flow (Overnight Rest)

1 Make Camp Everyone rolls Camping. No gear: disadvantage. At least one Survival check.
2 Lookout Secures camp. Failure gives disadvantage vs intruders.
3 Activities Non-lookouts perform one primary camp activity.
4 Sleep CON save vs activity DC. Bedroll/tent advantage, armor or lookout disadvantage.
5 Pack Up Put out the fire, strap on gear, and move out.
